Segway'n USA

Documenting my trip around the US on my very own Segway.

August 20, 2005

Well I made it 1200 miles already, and I passed through some interesting places on the way:

  1. Walla Walla, WA
  2. Magic City, ID
  3. Bountiful, UT
  4. Last Chance, CO
  5. Why, AZ
  6. Truth or Consequences, NM

July 14, 2005

I saw some Burma Shave style signs on the side of the road today:

Passing cars,
When you can't see,
May get you a glimpse,
Of eternity.

I definitely won't be passing any cars!

June 2, 2005

My first day of the trip! I can't believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I'm on a Segway, I wasn't able to bring a whole lot with me:

  • cell phone
  • iPod
    1. digital camera - Canon
    2. digital camera - Sony
    3. digital camera - Konica
  • a protein bar
Just the essentials. As Lao Tzu would have said, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway.
